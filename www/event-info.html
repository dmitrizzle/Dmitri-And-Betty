Where, when, what to wear & more

Wedding ceremony starts at 3:00PM sharp, on Wednesday, October 19th at Chiang Dao Nest 1. Please be on time & dress well. There will be a mini-bus to pick up guests from Chiang Mai.

Accomondations

Although the main event is during the afternoon and evening of the October 19th, we invite our guests to stay overnight with us at the resort.

Overseas guests: if you RSVP'd "yes", we assume you would like to stay overnight at The Nest. We have a hut with a comfy bed and A/C reserved for you (no charge). If you do not want to stay overnight, please let us know. We will make more exact arrangements closer to the event. There will be a mini-bus to pick you up from Chiang Mai on the 19th and take you back to Chiang Mai on the 20th.

Please make your own accommodation arrangements in Chiang Mai.

Local guests: if you live in Chiang Mai we can't promise you free accommodations at The Nest (there's limited space). Although you shouldn't have any issues booking a guesthouse close-by (most places are walking distance). We will let you know closer to the event if we have a spot for you.

Dress code

The dress code is semiformal.

Men suit, dress shirt, tie, dress shoes.

Women Gown, cocktail dress, or dressy separates.

No sandals for men. No running shoes. No Crocs. Be cool.

Food and drinks

The Nest has wonderful cooks on staff. As the guests arrive we'll have appetizer course followed by a table with cold-cuts and delicious foods that you have a few hours to enjoy.

There will be wine and beer on us (open-bar style) and you can also buy bottles of super fancy stuff should your heart desire that as well.

Gifts

We have a no gifts, no red pockets policy. All we want is your presence at our wedding.
